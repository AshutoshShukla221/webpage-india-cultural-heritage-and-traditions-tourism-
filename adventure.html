Adventure Activities

Himalayan Trekking

The Himalayas are a paradise for trekkers, offering everything from gentle walks through lush valleys to challenging ascents of snowy peaks. Popular treks like the Valley of Flowers in Uttarakhand, the Chadar Trek in Ladakh, and the trails around Himachal Pradesh provide stunning views and a chance to connect with nature in its purest form.

Whether you are a novice or an experienced mountaineer, the Indian Himalayas have a trail for you. The best seasons are typically spring (March-May) and autumn (September-November) when the weather is clear and the landscapes are at their most beautiful.

White Water Rafting in Rishikesh

Nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas, Rishikesh is renowned as the adventure capital of India, and for a good reason. The mighty Ganges River flows through it, creating thrilling rapids that are perfect for white-water rafting.
With various stretches ranging from Grade I to Grade IV+, there's a rafting experience for every level of adventurer. The season runs from September to June. As you navigate the roaring river, you're surrounded by pristine forests and sandy beaches, making it an adrenaline-pumping yet scenic experience.

Camel Safari in the Thar Desert

Experience the timeless allure of the desert with a camel safari in Rajasthan. Traverse the golden sands of the Thar Desert on the 'ship of the desert,' passing through remote villages and experiencing the stark beauty of the landscape.
Journeys can range from a single day trip to multi-day expeditions, culminating in a magical evening under a blanket of stars, enjoying traditional Rajasthani music and cuisine around a campfire. The best time to go is during the cooler months from October to March.

Scuba Diving in the Andaman Islands

The Andaman and Nicobar Islands are an untouched paradise with some of the best diving spots in South Asia. The crystal-clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and diverse marine life make for an unforgettable underwater adventure.
Havelock and Neil Islands are popular hubs for diving, with numerous PADI-certified centers offering courses for beginners and excursions for certified divers. Explore a world of colorful fish, majestic sea turtles, and stunning coral gardens. The best season for diving is from November to April.

Paragliding in Bir Billing

Recognized as one of the best paragliding sites in the world, Bir Billing in Himachal Pradesh offers an exhilarating experience of soaring through the skies. The launch site at Billing is perched at an altitude of 2400 meters, offering breathtaking views of the Dhauladhar mountain range.
Tandem flights with experienced pilots are available for everyone, making it a safe and accessible adventure sport. The flying season is from March to May and September to November, when thermal conditions are ideal for long flights.

Skiing in Gulmarg

Gulmarg, in the heart of Kashmir, is a premier destination for skiing in India. Known for its powdery snow and the Gulmarg Gondola, one of the highest cable cars in the world, it attracts winter sports enthusiasts from across the globe.
The slopes cater to all levels, from beginners to advanced skiers who can challenge the backcountry terrain. The stunning alpine scenery, with snow-laden pines and majestic peaks, makes skiing in Gulmarg a truly magical experience. The best season is from late December to mid-March.
